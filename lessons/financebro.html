What to do when you get in accidents

Right after the accident has occurred, check if all parties involved are injured. If anyone is dying or is in need of medical attention, call for an ambulance at 995 immediately. Do not move the bodies or vehicles while waiting for help.

  • Exchange information
  • Call police
  • Call insurance

Am I insured?

All NUS Students are insured by NUS Insurance. Aim to go to government hospitals as their bills are more claimable

  1. Visit MYCG website
  2. Make sure to collect as much evidence as possible from the accident site
  3. Ask medical certification and proof of diagnosis from your hospital
